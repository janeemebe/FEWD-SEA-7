If you are not running Windows Server 2012 R2 and SQL Server 2014 yet, it’s time to consider upgrading to take advantage of groundbreaking improvements in performance.

Support has either ended or will end soon for the following products, which means no more security updates, increased maintenance costs, and potential compliance concerns.

  • Threats to business applications such as payroll, HR, and accounting
  • Elevated danger for cybersecurity attacks on computer systems and databases
  • Non-compliance with PCI security standards for businesses that accept credit cards
  • Breaches of HIPAA data policies for businesses that handle client healthcare data

It can take up to 90 days for a small or midsize company to migrate, so don’t put your business at risk. Start planning your migration right away.
